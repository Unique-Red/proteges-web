About Us

In April 2022, a group of undergraduates, bonded by shared experiences and a passion for change, sat together reflecting on the challenges facing young Nigerians. As counselors in training at the University of Lagos, we were no strangers to the emotional toll of navigating life in today's world. Yet, we found ourselves struggling with our own mental health battles.

Around us, alarming reports of suicide cases echoed across campuses, revealing a silent crisis. It was in that pivotal moment that we realized mental well-being is not just essential but foundational to every human achievement.

We recognized the urgent need to help our peers understand this truth while breaking the cycle of stigma surrounding mental health. This awakening gave birth to Proteges Nigeria, a movement dedicated to destigmatizing mental health for our generation and those to come.

Our Journey

From that first conversation, we took bold steps into the world of mental health advocacy. We launched impactful projects that have reached over 4,000 young Nigerians, the most notable being our flagship initiative, the Mental Health Revolution Seminar. Through this annual event, we educate, empower, and mobilize individuals and communities to take meaningful action towards mental well-being.

A turning point came on December 23rd, 2023, when a deeply personal story revealed to us the gravity of unspoken emotional pain. This experience underscored the importance of speaking up and seeking help, reinforcing our commitment to create safe spaces where voices can be heard and healing can begin.

Who Are We?

Proteges is a mental health awareness initiative dedicated to promoting mental well-being as a critical component of overall health. We are led by counselors-in-training at the University of Lagos and are committed to SDG 3 (Good Health and Well-being). We work through advocacy, education, and action.

Our Mission

We are on a mission to extend helping hands to individuals navigating mental health struggles and empower them the little way we can to help them live holistically.

Our Vision

We envision a world where mental health is embraced without judgement, where seeking help is a sign of strength, and where every person feels valued, heard, and supported in their mental health journey.

Commitment to SDG 3

We are proud to align our efforts with United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 3 (SDG 3): Good Health and Well-being. By promoting mental health awareness, providing access to educational resources, and nurturing supportive environments,

we are actively contributing to the achievement of this global goal. We believe that by prioritizing mental well-being, we are not only building healthier individuals but also stronger communities.

Join the Revolution

We are Proteges Nigeria - 13 passionate counsellors in training, leading a mental health revolution. We invite you to join us on this journey of change, courage, and compassion.

Together, we can break the chains of stigma and create a future where mental well-being is understood, accepted, and celebrated..
